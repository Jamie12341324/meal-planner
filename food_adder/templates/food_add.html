Meal Planner

Meal Planner is a website you can use to make a meal out of different fruit and choose a mass for each type of fruit and then the website will tell you the potassium, magnesium, sodium and kcal for what you have selected. On this page can you can click the add button below a strawberry to add it to the list of fruits in the meal. If a fruit is in a meal and you don't want it there anymore you can click the delete button below the fruit. The reason why you would want to know how much potassium, magnesium, sodium and kcal are in a meal is because they effect health. Here is a list of health benefits.

  • potassium helps with metabolism and stress reduction
  • magnesium helps with bone, heart and mental health
  • sodium improves muscle and nervous function
  • kcal is involved in maintaining a balance between kcal consumed and energy expended
